Our Story

A sweet odyssey
that began with one store.

In 2013, Mithaas commenced its sweet odyssey with a single store in Ashirwad Complex — dedicated to preserving the authentic taste of Indian sweets and delicacies.

Sweetened by the smiles of satisfied customers, that one store grew into a network of 16+ outlets across the NCR. Every Mithaas counter is a small ode to our dedication: not just sweets, but moments of joy. Now, through our Frozen Food Division, we bring that same warmth to families and partners across the world.

Farm to Freezer

Five steps,
zero shortcuts.

Every box that leaves our facility passes through five quietly obsessive stages — designed to lock in flavour, aroma and nutrition without a single preservative.

01

Sourcing

Our team meticulously selects the finest, freshest ingredients — from desi ghee to seasonal produce — to craft exceptional culinary creations.

02

Sorting

A devoted team carefully sorts and grades each batch, keeping only the highest-grade ingredients for unparalleled quality.

03

Production

Modern technology meets time-honoured family recipes — every dish is crafted the way your grandmother would recognise it.

04

Packaging

IQF, Blast Freezing, Nitro-flushing and high-barrier films lock in freshness while minimising oxidation and moisture exposure.

05

Assurance

Processed in FSSC 22000 & USFDA certified units, every batch passes rigorous quality checks before it ever sees a freezer.

Frequently asked

Questions, answered.

Everything importers, distributors and retail buyers most often want to know about working with Mithaas Exports.

How does Mithaas handle and store frozen foods?
Mithaas rapidly cools freshly produced food using state-of-the-art freezing technology (IQF and blast freezing), storing it in cold-storage facilities at temperatures of −20°C and below to ensure optimal quality and freshness.
How long can frozen food be safely stored?
Frozen food can typically be safely stored for several months to a year, depending on the type of food and how well it's packaged. Mithaas products carry a 12-month shelf life at −18°C — recommended storage and handling guidelines are printed on every carton.
Are Mithaas's frozen Indian dishes as tasty and authentic as freshly prepared?
Absolutely. Our frozen dishes are crafted with authentic recipes and premium ingredients, ensuring every bite bursts with the rich, vibrant flavours of traditional Indian cuisine. With careful attention to detail and advanced freezing techniques, we deliver a dining experience that rivals homemade — offering convenience without compromising on taste or authenticity.
What are the minimum order quantities for export?
We work with importers, distributors and food-service partners across diverse volume requirements. MOQs vary by SKU and destination — please get in touch for a tailored quotation, sample shipment and HS-code documentation.
Can you private-label or co-brand for our retail chain?
Yes — we offer private-label and co-branded production for established retail and food-service partners, subject to volume thresholds and a standard product-development cycle.
