Our Story

A sweet idea
that grew into a family.

Mithaas began in 2012 as a single sweet shop with a quiet promise — that every ladoo, every barfi, every plate of chaat would taste of home.

Today, that promise lives on across 16+ outlets in the NCR — a chain of traditional sweet shops, namkeen counters and multi-cuisine vegetarian restaurants that serve North Indian thalis, South Indian dosas, Chinese favourites, and our signature desi ghee mithai, all under one warm, welcoming roof.

Headquartered in Noida, Uttar Pradesh, we're proudly 100% vegetarian — and proudly part of your festivals, your everyday cravings, and every occasion in between.
