At Mithaas Sweets and Restaurant Pvt. Ltd., your satisfaction matters to us. Because we deal in fresh, perishable food products, our cancellation and refund terms are designed to be fair to both our customers and the nature of the products we make. This policy explains when and how cancellations and refunds apply.

📌 Note: This policy should be read together with our Terms & Conditions. Orders placed through third-party delivery apps may be subject to that platform's own cancellation and refund rules.

SECTION 01

Our Approach

Every Mithaas product is freshly prepared with quality ingredients. Because food items are perishable and made-to-order, they cannot always be cancelled, returned, or resold once preparation has begun. We aim to handle every concern with care and resolve genuine issues fairly and promptly.

SECTION 02

Order Cancellations

  • Orders may be cancelled only before preparation has begun. To cancel, contact the outlet or our support team as soon as possible.
  • Once an order is prepared, packed, or dispatched, it can no longer be cancelled.
  • Customised orders, bulk orders, and gift hampers may have separate cancellation terms (see Section 07).
  • Mithaas reserves the right to cancel any order due to unavailability, pricing errors, or circumstances beyond our control — in which case a full refund will be issued.
SECTION 03

Refund Eligibility

Refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis. The general guidelines are:

✓ Refund May Apply
  • Order cancelled before preparation began.
  • Wrong item delivered.
  • Item damaged or spoiled on arrival.
  • Order not delivered / outlet unable to fulfil.
  • Duplicate or failed payment charge.
✕ Refund Not Available
  • Change of mind after preparation.
  • Order already consumed.
  • Delay caused by incorrect address/contact details.
  • Personal taste preferences.
  • Customised / perishable items (unless faulty).

  • Cancellations are accepted only if requested [X days/hours] before the scheduled delivery or pickup.
  • Any advance or deposit paid may be non-refundable once production has started. [Confirm deposit terms]

SECTION 08

Quality Concerns

Your experience matters to us. If you ever receive an item that doesn't meet our quality standards, please let us know promptly with details and photos where possible. We will review the matter and offer a suitable resolution — a replacement, refund, or store credit — based on the situation.
