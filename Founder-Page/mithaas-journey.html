Milestones

Our Growth Story

Every year brought new dreams, new outlets, new families we became a part of. Here's how we grew, one sweet milestone at a time.

2012
The First Sweet Shop Opens

Mithaas launches its first outlet at Ashirwad Complex, Sector 53, Noida — serving traditional Indian sweets with 5 dedicated team members.

Founded
2013
Casual Dining is Born

Expanding the vision beyond sweets, Mithaas opens its first casual dining restaurant — bringing multi-cuisine vegetarian meals to the table.

Dine-In Launch
2014
4 More Outlets Established

Growing demand fuels rapid expansion. By 2014, Mithaas had established 4 additional outlets, building a loyal community of sweet lovers across Noida.

Expansion
2016
In-House Factory & Bakery Division

A major milestone — Mithaas opens its own production factory in Noida and launches a dedicated bakery division, ensuring quality at every step.

Manufacturing
2018
10 Outlets Strong

Mithaas crosses the double-digit milestone — 10 outlets, a growing team, and an unwavering commitment to authentic flavours.

10 Outlets
2019
Beyond Noida — 12 Outlets

The Mithaas family expands beyond Noida for the first time, spreading the sweetness to neighbouring communities with 12 outlets.

NCR Expansion
2021
Automated Factory & R&D Push

The production facility is upgraded with state-of-the-art automation. Research & Development takes centre stage — new products, new possibilities.

Tech Upgrade
2023
Frozen & Export Markets

Mithaas enters the frozen food and export segments — taking authentic Indian flavours beyond borders to NRI families worldwide.

Global Reach
2024
Faridabad Entry — 16 Outlets Total

Mithaas expands into Faridabad, bringing the total outlet count to 16 and cementing its presence across the entire NCR region.

Faridabad
2025
Delhi, Ghaziabad & J&K

2025 marks Mithaas' most ambitious year — opening in Lajpat Nagar (Delhi), Ghaziabad, and even Vaishno Devi, Jammu. 25+ outlets. 1000+ employees. The journey continues.
