At Mithaas Sweets and Restaurant Pvt. Ltd., we are committed to delivering our sweets, snacks, and products as fresh as the moment they're made. Because we work with both perishable and packaged food items, our shipping methods are tailored to the type of product and your location — ranging from same-day hyperlocal delivery to international container exports. This policy explains each of our four shipping options.

📌 Note: Availability of each shipping method depends on the product type, your delivery location, and order size. Perishable items are limited to local and hyperlocal delivery, while packaged and frozen products can travel further.

Freshness and food safety are at the heart of everything we do. Depending on the product and shipping method, we use appropriate food-grade, sealed, insulated, or tamper-evident packaging to protect quality during transit.
