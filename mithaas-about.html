Our Story

How Mithaas Began

Raised in a close-knit family, Mr. Pushpendra Sharma developed a deep appreciation for the power of food to bring people together. For him, food was never just nourishment — it was a medium for connection, love, and shared joy.

After completing his B.Tech and MBA, he spent three years in the corporate world. Travelling the length and breadth of India, he discovered that food transcends boundaries — a universal language of celebration. Standing at a pivotal crossroads, he chose to follow his passion. In 2012, Mithaas was born in a single store in Ashirwad Complex, Sector 53, Noida.

By 2024, Mithaas had grown into a symbol of culinary excellence, quality, and tradition — living up to its heartfelt slogan.

Launched the first outlet in Sector 53, Noida — serving traditional Indian sweets.

2016

2021

Factory automated with state-of-the-art facilities and a strong focus on R&D.

Expanded into frozen & export markets, reaching families worldwide.
